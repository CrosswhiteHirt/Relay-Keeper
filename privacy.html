Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Relaykeeper handles information. Relaykeeper is designed as an offline single-player puzzle game.

Information We Collect

Relaykeeper does not require account registration and does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location, contacts, photos or payment information.

Local Game Data

The game may store progress, unlocked levels, stars, audio settings and similar gameplay preferences locally on your device. This data is used only to provide the game experience and is not sent to us.

Internet and Third Parties

The current version of Relaykeeper is intended to work offline. It does not include user accounts, cloud saves, online leaderboards, in-app purchases or advertising identifiers.

Children's Privacy

Relaykeeper does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Data Retention

Local game data remains on your device until you delete the app or clear the app data through the operating system.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes. The updated version will be identified by a new effective date.
